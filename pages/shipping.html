Policy

Shipping

How and when you'll get your order. No surprises at checkout.

Where we ship

United States and Canada. If you'd like us to ship somewhere else, email orders@teamsiddiqi.com and we'll see what we can do.

Rates and speeds

  • Free shipping on US orders over $100.
  • $6 flat standard shipping on US orders under $100.
  • $14 flat to Canada (duties may apply on arrival).

Standard delivery runs 3–5 business days after your order ships. We ship Monday through Friday, excluding holidays. Most orders leave our warehouse within 1–2 business days.

Tracking

You'll get a confirmation email when you order, and a second email with tracking as soon as the label is created. If you haven't seen tracking within 3 business days, check spam, then email us.

Lost or stuck packages

If tracking hasn't updated in 7 business days, email us — we'll open a claim with the carrier and either refund you or reship the order.

Wrong address

Orders with an incorrect or incomplete address that get returned to us will be reshipped once — you'll cover the second label. Please double-check before you submit.
