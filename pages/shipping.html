Policy

Shipping

How and when you'll get your order. No surprises at checkout.

Where we ship

United States and Canada. If you'd like us to ship somewhere else, reach out through our contact form and we'll see what we can do.

Rates

  • $9.99 flat standard shipping on all US orders.
  • $14 flat to Canada (duties may apply on arrival).

Tracking

You'll get a confirmation email when you order, and a second email with tracking as soon as the label is created.

Lost or stuck packages

If tracking hasn't updated in 7 business days, reach us through our contact form — we'll open a claim with the carrier and either refund you or reship the order.

Wrong address

Orders with an incorrect or incomplete address that get returned to us will be reshipped once — you'll cover the second label. Please double-check before you submit.

A note from Noah & Zach

We are new to this and working after school in between our sports — please allow up to 30 days for our initial launch orders. We hope to get more efficient soon.
