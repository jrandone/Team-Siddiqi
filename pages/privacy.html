Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect the information you share with us. Written plainly, in one sitting.

What we collect

When you place an order, donate, or subscribe to our list, we collect: name, email address, shipping address, phone number (for delivery updates only), and the items you purchased. When you pay, your card details are sent directly to Stripe — they never touch our servers.

Our site logs basic analytics: pages viewed, referring site, and anonymized device info, so we can see what's working.

How we use it

  • Fulfill your order — ship it, email you tracking, handle returns.
  • Send occasional emails about new drops (only if you opted in — one-click unsubscribe in every email).
  • Report aggregated sales to the cause we support, so donors can see the impact.
  • Detect fraud and keep the site secure.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.

Who we share it with

  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Vercel — site hosting and serverless functions.
  • Shipping carriers — only the address needed to deliver your order.
  • Email provider — if you join our list, your email is stored with our ESP for sending newsletters.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep your cart during a session. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

Your rights

You can ask us, any time, to see the data we have about you, correct it, or delete it. Email privacy@teamsiddiqi.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

If you're in the EU, UK, or California you have specific rights under GDPR / CCPA — those are covered by the same request.

Children

This site isn't directed to children under 13 and we don't knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll update the date below. Material changes will be announced on the homepage.
