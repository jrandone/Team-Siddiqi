Policy

All Sales Final

We don't accept returns, exchanges, or refunds on purchased items.

Why

Every dollar of our proceeds funds the Paani Project. Returns, exchanges, and restocking create costs — return shipping, handling, lost inventory — that would come out of the donation pool. A sales-final policy lets us give 100% to the cause.

Before you order

Please double-check size, color, and shipping address before checking out. If you're between sizes, reach out through our contact form and we'll do our best to help.

Damaged, defective, or wrong item

If your order arrives damaged or defective, or we sent the wrong item, reach out through our contact form within 7 days of delivery with your order number and a photo. We'll replace it at no cost, or refund that item if we can't.

Lost packages

If tracking hasn't updated in 7 business days, email us — we'll open a claim with the carrier and either reship the order or refund.

Donations

Donations are non-refundable. If you believe you donated in error, email us within 7 days and we'll review case-by-case.
