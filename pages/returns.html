Policy

Returns & Refunds

30 days, unworn, tags on. That's the short version.

What's eligible

You can return any piece within 30 days of delivery, as long as it's unworn, unwashed, and has the original tags attached. We'll refund the original card in full.

Donations are non-refundable. If you believe you donated in error, email us within 7 days and we'll review case-by-case.

How to return

  1. Email orders@teamsiddiqi.com with your order number.
  2. We'll email you a prepaid return label (US orders).
  3. Drop the package at USPS within 14 days of getting the label.
  4. Once we receive the piece, we'll inspect and refund within 5 business days. The refund typically shows on your card 3–10 days later, depending on your bank.

Exchanges

The fastest way to exchange is to return for a refund and reorder in the size or color you want. We'll fast-track the new order so you don't have to wait.

Damaged or wrong item

If anything arrives damaged or wrong, send a photo to orders@teamsiddiqi.com within 7 days of delivery and we'll replace it at no cost.

International returns

Return shipping from Canada is at your cost; once received, the refund is full for the goods and original shipping.
